After Completing the Study

I've chosen this profession for many reasons. First of all, I like that I can create whatever I want, I think. Secondly, it is this area that is progressing into something unpredictable that can simplify our lives in different aspects. Thirdly, I grew up surrounded by technology, so obviously I want to delve deeper into what surrounds me

And also, of course, again gain experience from them. Why do I need this experience? To open my own business. This is career growth...
