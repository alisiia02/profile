What is HZ university?



HZ University of Applied Sciences is located in Vlissingen and Middelburg in the Netherlands. It offers a variety of bachelor's and master's degree programs in fields like engineering, business, health, and more. HZ University is known for its practical, hands-on approach to education, emphasizing real-world experience and internships for students. It's a reputable institution for international students seeking high-quality education in a beautiful coastal region of the Netherlands.
